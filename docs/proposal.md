# CS224G Project Proposal — Gana

## Thesis
Advances in AI enable insurance carriers to replace large, opaque organizations with lean, transparent systems that automate repetitive and regulated workflows.

## Focus
Claims handling, from FNOL to resolution.

## Key Contributions
- Multimodal FNOL normalization
- Vision-based damage assessment
- AI-driven claims routing
- Fraud risk scoring
- Unified claim state abstraction
